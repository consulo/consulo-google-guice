This inspection reports any @com.google.inject.Inject annotations on non-'public' fields or methods. While Guice will be able to perform such injections in many cases (including most unit test scenarios), it may not be able to do so during deployment, due to security restrictions. In general, it is best to avoid such injection, either by public the injected field or method 'public' or, in the case of a field, creating a public setter and annotating it as @Inject.
